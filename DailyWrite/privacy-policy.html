Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

WriteHabit ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, profile photo (optional)
  • User Content: Essays, comments, and writing you create
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version (for troubleshooting)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app
  • To authenticate your account
  • To store your writing and preferences
  • To send notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve our services

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • iCloud Keychain (for authentication)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service providers (Firebase, Apple)
  • When legally required

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account
  • Export your writing
  • Opt-out of notifications

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure authentication to protect your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.

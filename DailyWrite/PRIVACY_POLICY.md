# Privacy Policy for DailyWrite

**Last Updated:** April 13, 2026

## Introduction

DailyWrite ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, username (when you create an account)
- **User Content**: Essays, drafts, comments, and other content you create
- **Profile Information**: Bio, social media links (optional)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, writing streaks, interaction patterns
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

### 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
- **Google Sign-In**: For account authentication (optional)
- **Apple Sign-In**: For account authentication (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

1. **To Provide Services**: Store and display your essays, enable social features
2. **To Improve App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
3. **To Communicate**: Send daily writing reminders (if enabled)
4. **To Sync Data**: iCloud backup for your essays

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
- Essays are encrypted in transit and at rest
- Passwords are never stored in plain text
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:
- With service providers (Firebase) to operate our app
- When required by law or legal process
- With your consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and all data
- Export your essays in multiple formats
- Control your content visibility (public/private)

## Children's Privacy

DailyWrite is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States (Firebase servers).

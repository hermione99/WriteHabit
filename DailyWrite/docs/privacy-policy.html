Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

  • Email Address: For account authentication
  • Username: For your public profile
  • Written Content: Your essays and drafts (stored securely)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the writing and social features
  • To sync your content across devices

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard encryption.

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your essays are only visible to others if you set them to public or friends-only.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your writings
